1. Introduction

Chalk Talk ("Chalk Talk," "we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that helps football coaches build playbook quizzes, design plays, and communicate with their players. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Chalk Talk mobile app (the "Service").

By creating an account or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to run the Service. Specifically:

  • Account information you provide during registration: your name, email address, hashed password, and role (Coach or Player).
  • Team and roster information including team name, competitive level (Youth, Middle School, or High School), position groups you belong to, and your team membership status.
  • Content you create in the app such as quiz questions, quiz submissions, drawn plays and formations, custom position labels, and direct or team messages you send.
  • Images you upload such as play diagrams attached to quiz questions or reference drawings, stored inside your account.
  • Basic technical data such as device type and app version, used only for troubleshooting.

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, camera roll, or advertising identifiers. We do not use cookies or third-party analytics trackers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To operate features you have chosen to use: quizzes, submissions, formations, playbooks, messaging, and exports.
  • To let coaches see submissions and communicate with players on their team, and to let players see quizzes and playbooks their coach has shared with them.
  • To send account-related emails such as password reset links.
  • To secure the Service, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your content to train machine-learning models. We do not show advertising in the app.

4. How We Share Information

Chalk Talk is a coaching tool, so certain information is intentionally visible to other users on your team:

  • Coaches can see the names, roles, and submissions of players who have joined their team.
  • Players can see quizzes, formations, and messages that their coach has shared with them or their position group.
  • Team members can see broadcasts sent by the coach to the team.

Beyond visibility within your team, we do not share your personal information with any third party except:

  • Service providers that host our infrastructure and deliver password reset emails, bound by contractual duties to keep your data confidential.
  • When required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users.

5. Minors and School-Age Athletes

Chalk Talk is designed for coaches at the Youth, Middle School, and High School levels, meaning some players using the app may be minors. If you are under 13 years old, you may only use Chalk Talk with the involvement and permission of a parent or legal guardian and under the direction of your coach.

Coaches are responsible for confirming that appropriate parental or guardian permission has been obtained before adding players under the age of 13 to a team on Chalk Talk. Parents or guardians may contact us at any time to review or request deletion of a minor's account.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Retention

We keep your account information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (see Section 8), we permanently delete your personal information, submissions, messages, formations, and, if you own a team, that team and its associated content, as described in Section 8.

Aggregated, non-identifiable statistics used to operate the Service may be retained indefinitely.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@chalktalkgo.com. We will respond within a reasonable time.

8. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your Chalk Talk account at any time directly inside the app:

  1. Open the Settings tab.
  2. Scroll to Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Type DELETE to confirm.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. When you delete your account, we remove:

  • Your user profile.
  • Your quiz submissions.
  • Messages you have sent or received.
  • Formations and quizzes you have created.
  • Your memberships in any team.
  • If you are the owner of a team, the team itself along with its roster, position groups, and pending invites.

If you cannot access the app to delete your account, email support@chalktalkgo.com from your account's email address and we will process the deletion on your behalf within 30 days.

9. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information. Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing. Authenticated sessions use signed JSON Web Tokens transmitted over HTTPS. Access to production data is limited to essential personnel.

No online service is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential.

10. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted service providers to run Chalk Talk:

  • Cloud hosting for our backend and database.
  • Resend for delivering transactional emails such as password reset links.
  • Expo for mobile app builds and delivery through the Apple App Store and Google Play.

These providers process personal information only on our behalf and only as needed to deliver the Service.

11. International Users

Chalk Talk is operated from the United States. If you use the app from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have data protection rules that differ from your country. By using the Service you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify you in the app or by email. Your continued use of Chalk Talk after any changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

Questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy? Email us at support@chalktalkgo.com.
